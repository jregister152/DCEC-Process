What is the problem you are trying to solve? - To bring awareness to the DCEC

Frame it as a design question? - How can the DCEC website promote their mission in a more effective manner?

State the ultimate impact you're trying to have? - Increase support and donations from the community - Make the DCEC more available to potential students.

What are some possible solutions? - More consice copy - User friendly design - Higher quality images - More focus, negative space, hierarchy, and organization - Better color pallete (86 Background).

Context and Restraints - Portraying special needs children in a non-discrimanetory manner - limited existing resources.

Restate Question 1 - How can the DCEC website be the flagship platform for effectively promoting their mission and raising awareness for support and donations from the community as well as for potential students.
