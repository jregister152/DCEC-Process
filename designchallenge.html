Design Challenge

The IDEO Human Centered Design Toolkit

What is the problem you are trying to solve?

Lack of awareness of the DCEC by the community.

Frame it as a design question.

How can the DCEC website promote their mission in a more effective manner?

Now, state the ultimate impact you're trying to have.

An increase in support and donations from the community and to make the resources of the DCEC more readily available to potential students.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

  • More consice copy
  • User friendly design
  • Higher quality images
  • More focus, negative space, hierarchy, and organization
  • Better color pallete
  • Get rid of the background

Finally, write down some of the content and constraints that you're facing.

  • Portraying special needs children in a non-discrimanetory manner
  • Limited existing resources

Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How can the DCEC website be the flagship platform for effectively promoting their mission and raising awareness for support and donations from the community as well as for potential students?
